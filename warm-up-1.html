Warm-up: Meet the Clock

Before we start our time adventure, let's take a look at the clock!

  • A clock has numbers 1 through 12 placed in a circle
  • The clock face is the round part where all the numbers are shown
  • The short hand is called the hour hand - it points to the current hour
  • The long hand is called the minute hand - it points to the minutes
